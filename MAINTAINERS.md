# Maintainers

Inseglet is created and led by its founding maintainer. This file records who is responsible for the
project and how decisions are made.

  - Contact: jamesliv@wonkido.net

## Governance

Inseglet currently follows a **benevolent‑maintainer** model: the lead maintainer has final say on
direction, scope, and releases. This keeps the immersive/spatial design coherent while the project is
young. Contributions are welcome and reviewed on their merits (see [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](CONTRIBUTING.md)).

As the contributor base grows, additional maintainers may be invited here, and governance may evolve
toward a shared model. Proposals for that are welcome in [Discussions](../../discussions).
